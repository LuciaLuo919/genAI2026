# Program Details

This program is designed to enhance your skills in generative AI. We welcome individuals with various backgrounds.

### Who Can Join?
- **Individuals with a programming background** are welcome to join the program.
- **Those without a background in programming**, who are eager to learn and comfortable with computers, are also encouraged to apply.

### Additional Opportunities
- Successful students may be invited to a **service learning course** in May.
- Those eligible for **STEM internships**, as part of a government-sponsored program, may be considered for hiring.

### Compensation for Research Students
Research students including PT and PhD students will have a **separate compensation scheme**.
